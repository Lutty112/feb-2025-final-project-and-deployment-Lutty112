About AquaScope

AquaScope is a web-based educational initiative that explores the application of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and Remote Sensing (RS) in water resource monitoring. As freshwater scarcity and pollution continue to rise globally, it's imperative to adopt modern tools for sustainable water management.
